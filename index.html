Nikola Tesla

The Man Who Electrified the World

Early Life

Nikola Tesla was born in 1856 in what is now Croatia. He was a brilliant inventor, known for his contributions to the development of alternating current (AC) electrical systems.

Key Inventions

  • Alternating Current (AC)
  • Tesla Coil
  • Wireless Transmission
  • Induction Motor

Tesla's Legacy

Despite dying nearly penniless, Tesla's ideas have shaped modern technology. He is now remembered as one of the most visionary scientists in history.
